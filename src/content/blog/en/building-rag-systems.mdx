# Building RAG Systems: What I Learned

Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG) combines the power of large language models with your own data. Here's what I learned building one.

## The Architecture

A RAG system typically has three phases:

1. **Ingestion** -- process and embed your documents
2. **Retrieval** -- find the most relevant chunks for a query
3. **Generation** -- use an LLM to synthesize an answer

## Key Takeaways

- Chunk size matters more than you think
- Embedding model choice impacts retrieval quality significantly
- Always evaluate with real user queries, not synthetic benchmarks
